Sayed Alim Farhad Hussain

Also Known As: Sayedalim510 | Gigi

Early Life

Born on September 25, 2002, in Demow, Sivasagar district of Assam, India, Sayed Alim Farhad Hussain grew up in a close-knit Muslim family. From a young age, he was deeply connected with nature and driven by creative expression.

Education

Sayed completed his schooling at St. John's School in Demow and pursued higher secondary studies at Science Academy, Demow. He later enrolled in Moran College, continuing his academic path while balancing a growing presence online.

Career & Passion

Sayed's journey into public life began through his passion for fitness and boxing. Around the age of 17, he started focusing on gym workouts and healthy living. In 2018, he created his Instagram account (@sayedalim510) and began posting travel reels, motivational quotes, and scenic photos.

He expanded into YouTube and Threads under the same handle. His content often centers around self-growth, nature, and spreading positivity.

Public Image

Known for the hashtags #orophile (lover of mountains) and ♎ (Libra), Sayed has positioned himself as a nature lover and digital storyteller. His work promotes mental strength, respect for all faiths, and uplifting messages.

Likes & Interests

Sayed admires actors like Salman Khan, Hrithik Roshan, and Vidyut Jammwal. His favorite food includes fried rice and pulao. He enjoys music by artists like Emiway Bantai and supports legendary sports icons like MS Dhoni, Messi, and Ronaldo.

Values

Despite his rising digital footprint, Sayed remains grounded. He avoids smoking and alcohol, respects spiritual values, and lives by the principle: “Whatever is good for your soul, do that.”
